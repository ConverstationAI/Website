About

We simulate conversation

Converstation AI is an AI company building simulation infrastructure for conversational intelligence. We develop platforms that let people practice, study, and improve communication — in clinical settings, research environments, and everyday life.

Our products are grounded in the science of human communication — drawing on linguistics, speech-language pathology, cognitive science, and conversational AI research. We build tools that take conversation seriously as a domain worth simulating with precision and depth.

We believe simulation is the missing layer in how humans and AI systems learn to communicate better. Our mission is to build that layer — and make it available to educators, researchers, and anyone who wants to practice before it counts.

"Conversation shapes outcomes in healthcare, research, and life. We build the AI infrastructure to simulate it, study it, and improve it."
